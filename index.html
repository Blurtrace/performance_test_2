Guess Who's Back?
Twenty øne piløts!!!

About who is Twenty øne piløts is an American musical duo from Columbus, Ohio, formed in 2009 by Tyler Joseph, Nick Thomas, and Chris Salih, later becoming a duo with Joseph and drummer Josh Dun in 2011


Mainstream Breakthrough and Blurryface (2015–2017)
  • Blurryface: Released in May 2015, the album debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200.
  • Grammy Success: "Stressed Out" won a Grammy Award for Best Pop Duo/Group Performance at the 59th Annual Grammy Awards.
  • "Heathens": Released in 2016 for the Suicide Squad soundtrack, the song peaked at No. 2 on the Billboard Hot 100.

Trench, Scaled and Icy, and Clancy (2018–Present)

  • Trench (2018): After a year-long hiatus, they released their fifth studio album, featuring "Jumpsuit" and "Nico and the Niners".
  • Scaled and Icy (2021): The sixth studio album was created during the COVID-19 pandemic and released in May 2021.
  • Clancy (2024): Their seventh studio album, released on May 24, 2024, concluded the elaborate narrative lore of the Dema/Trench era.
